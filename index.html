About Zclassic (ZCL)
Zclassic is a fair-launch, community-driven cryptocurrency founded in 2016 as a fork of Zcash to remove founder rewards, taxes, and restrictive licensing. Every block reward goes entirely to miners, ensuring an equitable, transparent network built on open-source principles. Governed by its users and secured through proof-of-work, Zclassic maintains a lean, resilient blockchain optimized for global accessibility. Operating under a permissive MIT-style license, the project empowers independent development and experimentation while preserving the integrity of its core chain through “opto-isolation,” a design that allows forks and innovations to thrive without risk to the main network. Its mission remains simple and enduring: a secure, decentralized, and truly fair digital currency for all.
