Zclassic: The First 100% Fair Mined PoW ZK-Snarks Decentralized Cryptocurrency

The Zclassic Genesis Block was mined Nov 6, 2016. All coins have been 100% PoW Mined with no coin sale, no ICO, no pre-mine, no tax etc.

The Zclassic chain has been forked several times. The first notable fork was the ZEN (Horizen) fork in May 2017 when the ZEN chain hard-forked off of Zclassic.
